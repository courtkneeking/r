Randomizer
Click on the arrow to begin. Next, select the place type and desired distance you wish you travel. The randomizer will then return a random place to you using your desired inputs.
The randomizer is a free online tool that uses your current location and specified inputs to return a random place in your area. Choose from restaurants, bars, clubs and more, get directions, read reviews from other randomized locations in your area and leave your own review. Last updated Jan 18, 2019.
